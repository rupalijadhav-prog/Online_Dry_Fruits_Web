Short-term benefits of free delivery

1. In the short-term, the greatest benefit of offering free delivery is a boost in sales.
2. If shoppers already like to buy your products, free delivery for existing customers will only entice more of them to return for another purchase.
3. In turn, that leads to greater order volumes and more profit.
